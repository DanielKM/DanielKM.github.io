Ecological and Environmental Advisory Committee (EEAC)

Citizen Appointee

The Ecological and Environmental Advisory Committee is a citizen appointed group of volunteers who advises Region of Waterloo Planning, Development and Legislative staff on environmentally sensitive issues.

This is my first year with EEAC and I hope to learn more about the environmental assessment process as well as ongoing development in my community. I also lend my environmental science expertise to the committee in particular related to phosphorus impacts.
