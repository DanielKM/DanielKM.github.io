About Me

About Me

Over a year ago I completed my master's of science in biology. It was loads of fun and I learned a lot, but my science career wasn't taking me in the direction I wanted. Three months ago I quit my job, and I have been learning programming and web development to switch to the tech field.

I am currently working as a contract Web Developer for a start-up in Waterloo, ON. I am also a volunteer in the Laurel Creek Citizens' Working Group, and the Ecological and Environmental Advisory Committee with the Region of Waterloo. I love backpacking, camping, biking, hiking and video games. I am hoping to take my love of the outdoors and combine it with web development to help to change the world.
