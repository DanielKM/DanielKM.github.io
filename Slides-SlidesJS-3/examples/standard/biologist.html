Natural Resource Solutions Inc.

Natural Resource Solutions Inc. (NRSI) is a mid-sized environmental consulting company based out of Waterloo, ON. NRSI staff work as part of multi-disciplinary teams for a diversity of clients in a wide range of sectors such as natural heritage planning, land development, renewable energy, environmental assessments and monitoring.

As a field biologist in the post-construction monitoring division, I performed a variety of ecological field surveys. These included bird and bat mortality surveys at wind turbines, searcher efficiency tests, and wildlife habitat mapping.
