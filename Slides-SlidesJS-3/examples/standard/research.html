The Soil Advocates

The Soil Advocates are a small environmental research firm based out of Uxbridge, ON. The primary goal of the company is to address the gap in scientific and technical knowledge that exists between many private sector markets and the current state of science.

My position as a research scientist was to act as a liaision between university staff and the company. I planned, conducted, and led field studies which examined nursery tree transplant success in damaged soils. My responsibilities also included data collection, management, and analysis as well as writing blog posts and maintaining technical documents.
